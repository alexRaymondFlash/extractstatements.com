FAQ

Common questions

Does my data get uploaded anywhere?
No. Everything runs locally on your computer. The app doesn't even have network code for uploading files - it's architecturally impossible for your data to leave your device.
What formats are supported?
PDF bank statements - both digital (text-based) and OCR'd scans. The app detects the bank automatically and uses a dedicated parser for each format. If your bank isn't specifically supported yet, a generic parser attempts extraction.
What if my bank isn't supported?
The app includes a generic parser that works with most standard statement formats. If it can't parse your statement, a built-in diagnostic tool generates a privacy-safe format fingerprint (no personal data) that you can send us to fast-track support for your bank.
Is there a subscription or per-document fee?
No. $99 one-time purchase. Process as many statements as you want, forever. Parser updates (new bank support) are included free.
What does the Excel output look like?
Two sheets: (1) Transactions - date, description, transaction type, debit/credit, running balance for every transaction. (2) Reconciliation - beginning balance, total credits, total debits, ending balance, computed vs. stated variance.
Can I use this for legal/forensic work?
That's exactly what it's built for. 100% local processing means no data handling concerns for attorney-client privileged material. The reconciliation sheet provides an auditable proof that the extraction ties to the source document.
Mac support?
Windows only right now. macOS is coming soon - the app is built on cross-platform technology (Tauri), so it's a matter of building and testing, not rewriting.
